ECE 370 Electromagnetic Fields and Waves (3)
Theory of electromagnetic fields and waves for engineering electromagnetics. Behavior of electrostatic and magnetostatic fields in media. Solutions to Maxwell's equations for time-varying electromagnetic fields and propagation of electromagnetic waves. Analysis and design of transmission-line systems. Prerequisties: ECE 210 and MATH 285.
